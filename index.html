Koh Losin Expedition

The Deep Gulf Frontier

September 17 - 21, 2026

The Ultimate Off-the-Grid Experience

Join Pattaya Scuba Adventures for the final trip of the 2026 season. Located 100km from shore, Koh Losin is Thailand's most remote diving pinnacle. Known for its iconic lighthouse and crystalline waters, it is the premier spot for Whale Shark encounters and untouched hard coral gardens.

📍 The Location

Deep Gulf of Thailand. No land, no crowds, just a lighthouse in the blue.

🐋 Marine Life

Frequent Whale Sharks, Guitar Sharks, and massive schools of Pelagics.

Expedition Itinerary

Day 1: Thursday, Sept 17 - Departure

Evening meetup at the pier in Songkhla. Boat briefing, equipment setup, and dinner as we begin the 10-hour steam to the deep Gulf.

Day 2 & 3: Sept 18 - 19 - The Pinnacle

4 Dives Daily. We wake up at the Losin Lighthouse. Exploring North and South pinnacles, staghorn coral forests, and keeping a keen eye out for Whale Sharks in the blue.

Day 4: Sunday, Sept 20 - Final Dives

3 Dives. Enjoy the final morning of remote diving before we start the journey back toward the mainland. Farewell sunset BBQ on the top deck.

Day 5: Monday, Sept 21 - Return

Morning arrival at the pier. Disembarkation and transfers to Hat Yai (HDY) for afternoon flights home.
